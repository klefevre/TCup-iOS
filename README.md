# TCup-iOS

Source code of the Edison server: [tCup-sensor](https://github.com/adrienjoly/tcup-sensor)

Instruction on how to make the box: [tCup instructables](http://www.instructables.com/id/Intel-IoT-Roadshow-Paris-tCup/)

### Usage

Select a kind of tea.<br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klefevre/TCup-iOS/master/Screenshots/01.png" width="320" height="568">

Then make boil your water at the perfect temperature.<br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klefevre/TCup-iOS/master/Screenshots/02.png" width="320" height="568">

Now you can infuse your tea ! Just wait the recommanded duration according to your tea choise.<br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klefevre/TCup-iOS/master/Screenshots/03.png" width="320" height="568">

Your tea is ready ! But wait... It's too hot to drink right now. Choose the temperature you want to drink your tea.<br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klefevre/TCup-iOS/master/Screenshots/04.png" width="320" height="568">

Wait for your tea to cool down at the perfect temperature.<br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klefevre/TCup-iOS/master/Screenshots/05.png" width="320" height="568">

Enjoy your tea !<br>
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/klefevre/TCup-iOS/master/Screenshots/06.png" width="320" height="568">
